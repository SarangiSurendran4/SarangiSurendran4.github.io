Selected Case Studies

1. Improving Feature Adoption Through Use-Case–Driven Content

Problem: Moderate adoption, high support tickets, and users unaware of feature use cases.

Action: Analyzed support tickets and calls, created use-case documentation, short tutorial videos, and community teaser posts to improve awareness. Collaborated with CSMs and internal teams to validate content.

Impact: Feature adoption increased, support tickets reduced, more engagement on community posts, and appreciation from PMs and managers.

2. Usability Testing with DAC Team

Problem: Limited understanding of how users interacted with documentation; some pods had low adoption.

Action: Conducted structured usability testing, collected insights, shared report, and created additional use-case articles, videos, and community posts.

Impact: Team implemented improvements, enhanced clarity and usability, improved adoption, and manager/team acknowledged efforts.

3. Broken Link & Asset Automation

Problem: Broken links and images across thousands of articles affecting search relevance and user experience. Manual checking was inefficient.

Action: Built Python-based crawler to check links and images weekly, auto-notifying owners for fixes.

Impact: Significant drop in broken links, improved search, reduced manual effort, and manager appreciation.

4. Jira Ticket & Article Aging Automation

Problem: Missed Jira tickets and outdated articles causing inefficiencies and user complaints.

Action: Automated Jira ticket aging report and article aging report; weekly notifications to writers; ensured content review cycle and SLA adherence.

Impact: Faster ticket closure, consistent article updates, improved accountability, and manager recognition.

5. Proactive Content Quality & Process Improvements

Problem: Manual audits, inconsistent content, outdated material.

Action: Partnered with UX writers, automated content audits, standardized practices, conducted knowledge sharing.

Impact: Consistent, up-to-date documentation, reduced manual work, improved UX and adoption, team recognition.

Diagnostics: Designing troubleshooting docs around user failure paths

Investigation

Diagnostics was a complex debugging feature. Technically accurate docs existed, but failed to map to how users actually behaved when systems broke. I listened to customer calls and shadowed L1 support to map exact failure paths.

System Realignment

Restructured the documentation hierarchy away from a "feature list" approach. Rebuilt the guide as a task-based troubleshooting flow, directly linked from every in-app error state.

Business Impact

The revised article became the primary reference for the support team. Strong post-launch adoption signaled that users could finally self-serve their debugging processes.

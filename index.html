Selected Case Studies

Improving Feature Adoption Through Use-Case–Driven Content

Problem: Moderate feature adoption, high support tickets, user confusion about use cases.

Action: Analyzed support tickets, calls, and queries. Created use-case documentation, tutorial videos, and community teaser posts. Collaborated with CSMs and internal teams.

Impact: Feature adoption increased, support tickets decreased, more community engagement, manager and PM appreciation.

Usability Testing with DAC

Problem: Limited understanding of how customers use support content; low adoption in some feature pods.

Action: Conducted structured usability test, created report with insights, recommended improvements, created additional articles, videos, and community posts.

Impact: Enhanced clarity, usability, and feature adoption; manager and team appreciated initiative.

Broken Link & Asset Automation

Problem: Broken links/images across thousands of articles affecting search and user experience; manual checking impossible.

Action: Built Python-based crawler to check links/images weekly and automatically notify owners for fixes.

Impact: Significant drop in broken links, improved search, reduced manual effort, manager appreciation.

Jira Ticket & Article Aging Automation

Problem: Missed Jira tickets and outdated articles affecting user experience; manual tracking inefficient.

Action: Automated Jira ticket and article aging reports to notify writers weekly; enforced review practices.

Impact: Tickets tracked and closed faster, articles updated regularly, improved accountability, manager recognition.

Proactive Content Quality & Process Improvements

Problem: Manual audits, inconsistent content, outdated material affecting user experience.

Action: Partnered with UX writers, automated content audits, standardized practices, knowledge sharing sessions.

Impact: Consistent, up-to-date documentation, reduced manual work, improved UX and adoption, team recognized efforts.

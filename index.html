Broken Link & Asset Automation

Context: Support site had thousands of articles. Broken links and outdated assets affected search relevance and user experience.

Problem: Frequent broken link reports, inefficient manual audits, existing tools limited.

Approach: Built Python-based broken link/image checker, repository to track articles and ownership, automated weekly notifications to pod owners.

Execution: Scripts to scan weekly, auto-notifications, dashboards to track fixes.

Impact: Significant drop in broken links, improved search and user experience, reduced manual effort, appreciated by manager.

Jira Ticket & Article Aging Automation

Context: Writers missed tickets, managers tracked manually; articles outdated, causing support complaints.

Problem: Missed tickets, inefficient tracking, 2000+ articles not updated, inconsistent content.

Approach: Automated Jira ticket aging report (tickets older than 5 days). Created article aging report, weekly notifications to writers, enforced content review practice.

Execution: Scripts to sync weekly, automated reports with article title, link, age, assigned writer.

Impact: Faster ticket closure, articles maintained <120 days, improved content quality, manager/team appreciated automation.

Usability Testing with DAC Team

Context: Support content (articles, videos, posts) needed evaluation for usability and effectiveness; feature pods like AVR had low adoption.

Problem: Limited understanding of customer interaction with content, gaps in guidance, low adoption.

Approach: Conducted structured usability testing with DAC, asked 8 targeted questions about documentation, usage, clarity, and adoption.

Execution: Compiled insights into usability report, suggested use-case articles, videos, community posts for engagement.

Impact: Actionable insights, implemented improvements, increased adoption, positive recognition from team and manager.

Proactive Content Quality & Process Improvements

Context: Maintaining quality at scale challenging, manual audits, inconsistent content across pods.

Problem: Inefficient manual processes, inconsistencies, outdated content affecting users.

Execution: Scripts, dashboards, weekly checks, knowledge sharing sessions.

Impact: Consistent, up-to-date documentation, reduced manual effort, improved search and UX, recognized by managers and peers.

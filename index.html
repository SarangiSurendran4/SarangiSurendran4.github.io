Selected Case Studies

Improving Feature Adoption Through Use-Case–Driven Content

+

Action: Analyzed support tickets, calls, and queries. Created user-friendly use case docs, tutorial videos, and community teaser posts. Collaborated with CSMs and internal teams.

Impact: Feature adoption increased, support tickets decreased, more community engagement, and appreciation from PMs and managers.

Usability Testing with DAC

+

Problem: Limited understanding of how customers use support content; some feature pods had low adoption.

Action: Conducted structured usability test with DAC, created report with insights, recommended improvements, created additional use-case articles, videos, and community posts.

Impact: Team implemented improvements, enhanced clarity and usability, improved adoption, and manager/team acknowledged initiative.

Broken Link & Asset Automation

+

Problem: Broken links/images across thousands of articles affecting search and user experience; manual checking impossible.

Action: Built Python-based crawler to check links/images weekly and automatically notify owners for fixes.

Impact: Significant drop in broken links, improved search, reduced manual effort, and manager appreciation.

Jira Ticket & Article Aging Automation

+

Problem: Missed Jira tickets and outdated articles affecting user experience; manual tracking was inefficient.

Action: Automated Jira ticket aging report and article aging report to notify writers weekly; enforced content review practices.

Impact: Tickets tracked and closed faster, articles updated regularly, improved accountability, and manager appreciation.

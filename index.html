Selected Case Studies

Improving Feature Adoption Through Use-Case–Driven Content

Problem: Moderate feature adoption, high support tickets, and user confusion about feature use cases.

Action: Analyzed support tickets, calls, and queries. Created user-friendly documentation, tutorial videos, and community teaser posts. Collaborated with CSMs and internal teams.

Impact: Feature adoption increased, support tickets decreased, community engagement rose, and PM/manager appreciation received.

Usability Testing with DAC

Problem: Limited understanding of how customers use support content; some pods had low adoption.

Action: Conducted structured usability testing, created insights report, recommended improvements, authored use-case articles, videos, and engaging posts.

Impact: Clarity and usability improved, feature adoption increased, and initiative recognized by team and manager.

Broken Link & Asset Automation

Problem: Broken links and outdated assets affecting thousands of articles, harming search and UX; manual checking was impossible.

Action: Built Python-based automation to check links and images weekly, notifying assigned owners automatically.

Impact: Significant drop in broken links/assets, improved search and UX, reduced manual work, manager appreciation.

Jira Ticket & Article Aging Automation

Problem: Missed Jira tickets and outdated articles; manual tracking inefficient; over 2,000 articles required proactive review.

Action: Automated weekly Jira ticket and article aging reports, notifying writers to update content and close tickets.

Impact: Tickets tracked and closed faster, articles updated regularly, accountability improved, manager/team recognized initiative.

Proactive Content Quality & Process Improvements

Problem: Manual audits, content inconsistencies, and outdated documentation across multiple pods.

Action: Partnered with UX writers, automated audits, standardized practices, conducted knowledge sharing.

Impact: Consistent, up-to-date documentation, reduced manual work, improved UX and adoption, team acknowledged efforts.

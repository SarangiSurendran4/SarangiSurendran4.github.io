About Me

I am an Information Developer with 2.6 years of experience documenting enterprise SaaS products at Whatfix. I currently work as an ID2 (Information Developer 2), with close to one year in this role.

My work focuses on translating complex product workflows into clear, structured documentation for end users, support teams, and internal stakeholders. I strongly believe that good documentation is not just informative—but actionable, discoverable, and visually intuitive.

How I Work

I collaborate closely with Product Managers, Developers, Engineers, Designers, Customer Success Managers, Support teams, and DAC teams to understand product intent and user pain points.

My documentation process is research-driven and user-focused. I work directly with the product, test workflows, identify gaps, and structure content so users can quickly understand, act, and succeed without external assistance.

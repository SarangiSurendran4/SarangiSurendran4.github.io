Case Studies

Feature Adoption Through Use-Case Content

Problem: Moderate adoption, high support tickets, and user confusion about feature use cases.

Action: Analyzed tickets and calls, created user-friendly docs, tutorial videos, and community teaser posts. Collaborated with CSMs and internal teams.

Impact: Increased feature adoption, reduced support tickets, more engagement, and manager appreciation.

Usability Testing with DAC

Problem: Limited understanding of how customers use support content; low adoption in some pods.

Action: Conducted structured usability tests, created reports with actionable insights, recommended improvements, created new content.

Impact: Improved clarity, adoption, and content effectiveness; team and manager appreciated initiative.

Broken Link & Asset Automation

Problem: Thousands of broken links/images affecting search and user experience; manual checking impossible.

Action: Built Python crawler to check links weekly and notify owners automatically.

Impact: Significant drop in broken links, improved search, reduced manual effort, manager appreciation.

Jira Ticket & Article Aging Automation

Problem: Missed tickets and outdated articles affected user experience; manual tracking was inefficient.

Action: Automated weekly reports for ticket aging and article review, notifying assigned writers to ensure updates and closures.

Impact: Faster ticket resolution, up-to-date articles, improved accountability, manager appreciation.

Proactive Content Quality Improvements

Problem: Manual audits and inconsistencies across pods; outdated material impacted UX.

Action: Partnered with UX writers, standardized content, automated audits, conducted knowledge sharing sessions.

Impact: Consistent, up-to-date content, reduced manual work, improved UX and adoption, team recognized efforts.
